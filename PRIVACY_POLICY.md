## The short version

- Your answers stay on your device, encrypted, and — when you sign in — sync to our database in **Sydney, Australia**.
- Your data **never leaves Australia**.
- **Only you can see your data.** We cannot read your answers through the app, and database rules restrict every record to the account that created it.
- There is **no advertising, no analytics, and no tracking** of any kind.
- You can **delete your account and all your data at any time**, from inside the app. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

## What we collect

**Account information:** your email address and a password (the password is stored only in securely hashed form by our database provider).

**Assessment information:** everything you type into the tool — this can include your name, date of birth, NDIS number, disability and health conditions, medications, support needs, mental health information, and notes from support people or a psychologist. This is **sensitive health information** under the Privacy Act 1988, and we treat it that way.

We collect **nothing else**. No location, no contacts, no advertising identifiers, no usage analytics.

## Why we collect it (APP 3, APP 6)

For one purpose only: so the app can work — saving your preparation notes, letting you resume on any device you sign in on, and generating your report. We do not use your information for marketing, research, profiling, or any secondary purpose, and we do not enrich it with data from anywhere else.

## Where your data lives (APP 8 — no overseas disclosure)

Your data is stored in two places:

1. **On your device**, in an encrypted database that only the app can read.
2. **On our server**, hosted by Supabase in Amazon Web Services' Sydney region (ap-southeast-2), Australia.

We do not store or process your data outside Australia, and we do not use any overseas service to handle it. Reports you generate (PDF or text files) are created on your device and go only where you choose to send them.

## Who can see your data (APP 6, APP 11)

**You.** Each account's data is locked to that account by database-level security rules (row level security). Other users cannot access it. We do not have a way to browse your answers through the app.

Like any hosting arrangement, the database provider's infrastructure staff could theoretically access stored data under their own strict controls; Supabase's systems encrypt data at rest and in transit. We will never voluntarily disclose your information to anyone, and we would only respond to a legal compulsion (such as a court order) after verifying it is valid, and we would tell you unless the law prevented us.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information. Ever.

## How your data is protected (APP 11)

- Encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS) between your device and the server.
- Encrypted at rest on the server, and in the app's on-device database.
- Sign-in tokens are held in your device's secure keychain, not in ordinary app storage.
- Passwords are hashed, never stored in readable form.

No system is perfectly secure, but there is no advertising or analytics code in this app to leak data sideways — the only network connection the app makes is to our Sydney database.

## Your choices and rights (APP 12, APP 13)

- **See your data:** everything you have entered is visible in the app, and you can export it as a PDF, text file, or data file at any time.
- **Correct it:** edit anything directly in the app.
- **Delete it:** Account screen → Delete my account. This permanently removes your account and every answer from our server and your device. There is no retention period and no backup we keep of deleted accounts beyond the provider's short-lived rolling database backups, which expire automatically.
- **Use it without an account:** you may stop at any time; closing the app costs you nothing but unsaved work.

## Children

The app is intended for adults (18+) preparing for NDIS planning, or for use with the support of an adult carer or clinician.
